The Sugarbush

Eastern Ontario
Sugar Maple

The property runs about 300 taps across 100 trees. Sap is collected under vacuum through a mainline system into a central releaser tank, then transferred to a 1000L tote for RO concentration before the evaporator.

The evaporator is a wood-fired continuous-boil setup — a 24×48×8" stainless pan over a fire brick firebox, loaded with sap in the morning. Syrup is drawn at the end of the day, not continuously. The result is a darker, more complex product than most commercial operations produce.

There's no automation on the boiler. That part is still manual.
